Ahwal e Shahkot – Child Safety & CSAE Standards

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Ahwal e Shahkot has a strict zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, behavior, or activity that sexually exploits or harms minors is absolutely prohibited and will result in immediate account removal and reporting to authorities.

2. Prohibited Content

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Any content that sexualizes individuals under 18
  • Sharing or distributing harmful content involving children

3. In-App Reporting

Users can report any suspicious or harmful content directly within the Ahwal e Shahkot app using the "Report" button available on every post, profile, and message. All reports are reviewed by our moderation team within 24–72 hours.

4. Moderation & Action

  • Reported content is immediately hidden pending review
  • Confirmed violations result in permanent account ban
  • Urgent/emergency reports are escalated immediately
  • All violations are reported to relevant authorities

5. Cooperation with Authorities

Ahwal e Shahkot fully cooperates with law enforcement and national/regional authorities. We will provide all necessary information and evidence when legally required to do so.

7. Compliance

Ahwal e Shahkot complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations. We report violations to regional and national authorities as required by law.
