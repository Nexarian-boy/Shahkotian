Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains what information NewShahkotApp collects, how we use it, and your choices.

2. Information We Collect

a) Information you provide: account details (phone, email), profile data, posts, messages, and reports.

b) Usage & device data: analytics, crash reports, device identifiers, and IP address.

c) Location: if you grant permission, we may use approximate or precise location for location-based features.

3. How We Use Information

To provide and improve the app, authenticate users, enable features (chat, posts, location-based services), moderate content, and communicate with you.

4. Ads & Third Parties

We display ads served by third parties (for example, Google AdMob). These providers may collect device identifiers and usage information to serve personalized ads. See their privacy policies for details.

We may use third-party analytics and crash-reporting services (e.g., Google Analytics, Sentry) which collect usage data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers who operate services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, email), or when required by law.

6. Your Choices

You can delete your account, opt out of personalized ads in device/ad settings, and disable location permissions in your device settings.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect data, but no method is perfectly secure.

8. Children

The app is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.
